My Journey into Web Programming.

It has taken me 10 years to embrace my love of web development and dedicate myself to the craft. I signed up with General Assembly in Sydney to learn the skills needed to work tint the field hat makes me happy.

The journey has been like a roller coaster ride. Filled with anxiety when approaching new hurdle, absolute rapture as the understanding of a technology crystalized and devastating lows when staggering through minefields of realised misconceptions. Now that my formal training is complete, I can't wait to apply the knowledge I've amassed over the past months, but also to keep learning and growing as a part of the programming community.

The programming community in Sydney is filled with amazing people and I am fortunate to have attended the Rails Girls event at NineFold. The monthly Ruby On Rails DevHub and Ruby On Rails Oceania are essential for meeting awesome programmers who are always willing to help newbie programmers like myself.
