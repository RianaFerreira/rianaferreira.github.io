My journey into web programming

Into the web

It has taken me 10 years to embrace my love of web development and dedicate myself to the craft. After studied computer science I followed the path of Systems Analysis, working closely with development teams.

I've been inspired by so many brilliant programmers who are passionate about their work. Their enthusiasm is infectious and I'll happily spend my time on projects that I know will have a positive impact on the user experience.

One step at a time

The journey has been like a roller coaster ride. Filled with anxiety when approaching new hurdle, absolute rapture as the understanding of a technology crystallized and devastating lows when staggering through minefields of realised misconceptions.

Now that my formal training is complete, I can't wait to apply the knowledge I've amassed over the past months, but also to keep learning and growing as a part of the programming community.
